Car Loan - Islamic Interest-Free Financing

Akhuwat Foundation car loan aap ko gari kharedne ke liye bina sood ka qarz faraham karta hai. Ye loan un logon ke liye hai jo rozana safar ke liye gari ki zaroorat rakhte hain lekin conventional bank loans lena nahi chahte kyunkeh un mein sood (interest) hota hai jo Islam mein haram hai.

Hamara car loan program specially designed kia gaya hai taake aap Islamic tareeqe se gari khared saken. Hum aap ko gari kharedne ke liye full amount ya partial amount de sakte hain, aap ki zaroorat ke mutabiq.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Pakistani citizen with valid CNIC
  • Age between 21 to 60 years
  • Minimum monthly income: Rs. 25,000
  • No default history with any financial institution
  • Two guarantors required
  • Vehicle should not be more than 5 years old (for used cars)

Required Documents

  • CNIC copy (applicant and guarantors)
  • Last 3 months salary slip or income proof
  • Utility bill for address verification
  • Two passport size photographs
  • Vehicle quotation from authorized dealer (for new cars)

Islamic Perspective:

Islam mein transportation ke liye gari ki zaroorat ko aham samjha gaya hai, lekin sood (interest) ko haram qarar diya gaya hai. Akhuwat Foundation aap ko halal tareeqe se gari kharedne ka moqa deta hai. Quran mein hai: "Allah has permitted trade and has forbidden interest." (Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:275). Isi principle par hum kaam karte hain.
