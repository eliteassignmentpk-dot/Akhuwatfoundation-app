About Akhuwat Foundation

Hamara mission: Ghareebi khatam karna bina sood ke qarze ke zariye, Islamic usoolon par amal karte hue.

Our Mission & Vision

Akhuwat Foundation was established in 2001 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a poverty-free society based on the principles of brotherhood, equality, and social justice. Humara maqsad hai ke Pakistan mein har aisi family jo financially mahroom hai, unko bina sood ke qarze provide karna taake woh apne paon par khare ho saken.

وَتَعَاوَنُوا عَلَى الْبِرِّ وَالتَّقْوَىٰ ۖ وَلَا تَعَاوَنُوا عَلَى الْإِثْمِ وَالْعُدْوَانِ
"And cooperate in righteousness and piety, but do not cooperate in sin and aggression." (Surah Al-Ma'idah, 5:2)

Yeh ayat hamare kaam ka asal maqsad bayan karti hai. Hum maashray ke logon ko milkar ek doosre ki madad karte hain taake sab aik saath tarakki kar saken.

Akhuwat ka model conventional banking system se bilkul different hai. Yahan par hum qarze dene ke liye masajid, churches, aur temples ka istemal karte hain, taake qarz lene wala apne aap ko comfortable feel kare. Is tareeqe se hum ne 99% se ziada recovery rate hasil ki hai, jo ke dunia bhar mein ek misaal hai.

Our Core Values

Islamic Brotherhood

"Akhuwat" ka matlab hi hai bhai chara. Hum isi principle par kaam karte hain ke sab musalman aik doosre ke bhai hain.

Interest-Free

Hum sood (interest) ko haram mantay hain. Isliye hamare tamam qarz bilkul bina sood ke hote hain.

Community-Based

Humara model community par based hai. Local communities hi hamare branches hain.

Compassion

Har qarz dene wala apna paisa dil se deta hai, investment ki soch se nahi.

مَّثَلُ الَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَالَهُمْ فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ كَمَثَلِ حَبَّةٍ أَنبَتَتْ سَبْعَ سَنَابِلَ فِي كُلِّ سُنبُلَةٍ مِّائَةُ حَبَّةٍ ۗ وَاللَّهُ يُضَاعِفُ لِمَن يَشَاءُ
"The example of those who spend their wealth in the way of Allah is like a seed which grows seven spikes; in each spike is a hundred grains. And Allah multiplies for whom He wills." (Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:261)

Yeh ayat hamare donors aur qarz dene walon ke liye targheeb hai. Jo Allah ki raah mein deta hai, Allah use kayi guna ziada karke wapas karta hai.

Our Impact

Ab tak Akhuwat Foundation ne Pakistan bhar mein 4 million se ziada logon ko bina sood ke qarze provide kiye hain. Hamari loan recovery rate 99.9% hai, jo ke dunia ki kisi bhi microfinance institution se better hai. Hum ne sirf loans hi nahi diye, balke logon ki zindagiyan badli hain.

Akhuwat ka model ab internationally bhi pehchana ja raha hai. Hum ne apne model ko Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, aur Kenya mein bhi implement karna shuru kia hai. Hamara maqsad hai ke purey world mein poverty khatam ki jaaye bina sood ke qarze ke zariye.
