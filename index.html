Introduction to Akhuwat Foundation

Akhuwat Foundation is a pioneering non-profit organization dedicated to providing interest-free microfinance to empower the underprivileged in Pakistan. Established on the principles of Qarz-e-Hasna (beautiful loan), our mission is to alleviate poverty through Islamic social finance. Hum bina sood ke qarze ke zariye ghareeb logon ko unki izzat bachate hue unki maali zaroorat poora karte hain.

Our model is based on the Islamic concept of brotherhood (Akhuwat) and social responsibility. We believe in empowering individuals through financial inclusion while maintaining their dignity. Qarz-e-Hasna is not just a loan but a trust between the lender and borrower, rooted in mutual respect and Islamic values.

مَن ذَا الَّذِي يُقْرِضُ اللَّهَ قَرْضًا حَسَنًا فَيُضَاعِفَهُ لَهُ أَضْعَافًا كَثِيرَةً
"Who is it that will loan to Allah a beautiful loan which Allah will multiply for him many times over?" (Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:245)

Yeh ayat hamare kaam ki asal bunyad hai. Qarz-e-Hasna ka tasawwur Islam mein ek azeem aur sawab ka kaam hai jahan aap Allah ki raah mein dete hain aur uska badla aapko Allah ki taraf se milta hai.

Islamic Perspective on Interest

يَمْحَقُ اللَّهُ الرِّبَا وَيُرْبِي الصَّدَقَاتِ

"Allah destroys interest and gives increase for charity." (Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:276)

Islam mein sood (interest) ko sakht mana gaya hai kyunkeh yeh ghareeb logon par zulm hai aur maashray mein na-insaafi barhata hai. Bina sood qarz dekar hum asal Islamic ehsaas e hamdardi ka izhaar karte hain.

Loan Categories

Select from our range of interest-free loan categories designed to meet various needs while adhering to Islamic principles.

Car Loan

Bina sood gari loan zaruratmand logon ke liye jo rozana safar ya kaam ke liye gari ki zaroorat rakhte hain.

House Loan

Ghar banane ya kharedne ke liye bina sood qarz taake har kisi ko apna ghar hasil karne ka moqa mile.

Business Loan

Chotay karobar aur dukaan ke liye bina sood maali imdaad taake log apne paon par khare ho saken.

Personal Loan

Zaroori shadi, ilaj ya kisi aur zaroori maali zaroorat ke liye bina sood qarz ki facility.

Student Loan

Talba ko unki taleem ke liye financial support taake koi bhi lachaar talib-e-ilm taleem se mahroom na rahe.

Umrah Loan

Umrah ki khwahish rakhne walon ke liye bina sood qarz ki facility taake har kisi ko is ibadat ka moqa mile.

About Akhuwat

Akhuwat Foundation is Pakistan's largest interest-free microfinance organization. We are committed to poverty alleviation through Islamic social finance based on the principles of Qarz-e-Hasna.
