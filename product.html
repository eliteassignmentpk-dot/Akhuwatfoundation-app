Financial Products

Our Financial Solutions

Akhuwat Foundation ke tamam financial products Islamic shariah ke mutabiq hain. Hum conventional banking system se bilkul different hain. Humara focus sirf paise dena nahi, balke logon ki zindagiyan better banane par hai. Yahan har product ka design aisi tarah se kia gaya hai ke wo ghareeb insaan ki actual zaroorat ko poora kare.

Humare products mein koi hidden charges nahi hain, na hi koi compound interest. Humari fees clear aur transparent hain. Aap ko pata hoga ke aap ne kitna qarz liya hai aur kitna wapas karna hai. Is transparency ki wajah se hi hamare 99% se ziada clients apna qarz time par ada karte hain.

Qarz-e-Hasna

Yeh hamara basic product hai. Is mein aap ko bina sood ka qarz milta hai jo aap fixed monthly installments mein wapas karte hain. Loan amount Rs. 50,000 se Rs. 5,000,000 tak ho sakta hai.

Key Feature: No interest, no hidden charges

Family Support Loan

Khandani zaroorat ke liye special loan. Is mein aap apni family ki health, education, ya kisi emergency ke liye loan le sakte hain. Processing fast hoti hai.

Key Feature: Quick processing for emergencies

Education Financing

Talba ke liye special loan program. Is mein student ko directly loan milta hai jo wo apni education complete karne ke bad job hasil karne par wapas karega.

Key Feature: Grace period until graduation

Small Business Loan

Chotay karobar walon ke liye special product. Is mein business plan submit karna hota hai. Loan approval ke bad business training bhi di jati hai.

Key Feature: Business training included

Housing Finance

Ghar banane ya kharedne ke liye long-term loan. Ye loan 15 saal tak ke liye mil sakta hai. Monthly installment aap ki affordability ke hisab se set ki jati hai.

Key Feature: Long repayment period

Emergency Medical Loan

Health emergencies ke liye special quick loan. Documentation minimal hoti hai taake time na waste ho. Hospital bills direct pay kiye ja sakte hain.

Key Feature: 24-hour approval possible

How Our Products Differ

Akhuwat ke products conventional bank loans se kai tareeqon se different hain:

  • No Interest: Hamare products mein sood bilkul nahi hota. Jo amount aap lete hain, wohi amount wapas karte hain.
  • Community-Based: Hum masajid, churches aur temples mein centers rakhte hain, banks mein nahi.
  • Trust-Based: Hum aap par bharosa karte hain. Collateral ki zaroorat nahi hoti.
  • Transparent: Sab kuch clear hai. Koi hidden charges nahi.
  • Supportive: Loan ke sath sath hum aap ko training aur guidance bhi dete hain.

Important Islamic Principle:

Islam mein qarz dena ek azeem sawab ka kaam hai. Quran mein hai: "Who is it that will loan to Allah a beautiful loan which Allah will multiply for him many times over?" (Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:245). Isi principle par hum kaam karte hain. Aap ka qarz aap ki aakhirat ke liye bhi investment hai.
